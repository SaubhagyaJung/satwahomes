ABOUT SATWA


We create houses that go beyond conventional architecture. These are spaces that dissolve into nature, not only complementing it but also becoming part of it. Our team brings together architects, designers, and visionaries who believe in minimalism as a way of life.


Our vision is that true luxury lies in simplicity, harmony, and attention to detail. Each of our projects is a unique story created for those who seek a fine balance between modernity and nature. For those who are looking for something more than just a house.
